About ValidusEduTech

At ValidusEduTech, we believe in learning that shapes the whole personality. Our unique initiatives, Students' KATTA and S.P.A.R.K., are designed to give students the edge they need.

Students' KATTA: Forget rigid classrooms! Students' KATTA brings students together in a relaxed cafe setting for about two hours. Through informal 'Katta Talks' and 'Katta Stories' on non-academic topics chosen by the students themselves, they find a safe space to express freely, share experiences, and learn from their peers. This initiative strongly focuses on building a sense of community and introducing the vital concept of networking, helping students develop communication and interpersonal skills crucial for their future.

S.P.A.R.K. (Seek, Persist, Adventure, Reflect, Kindle): Our outdoor experiential learning program focused on career orientation. It helps students explore possibilities and build valuable life skills outside the traditional classroom setting.

ValidusEduTech provides platforms where students can explore diverse interests, build confidence, and develop essential soft skills alongside their academic journey.
